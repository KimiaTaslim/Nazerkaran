About Us

Nazerkararan Company was established in 1991 with the aim of providing engineering and inspection services based on national and international standards and cooperating in the drafting and dissemination of the culture of using these standards. Due to the importance and need for inspection, supervision and quality control of equipment under construction in refineries, petrochemical and gas industries, the company focused its main activity on this field. With the latest methods, world-class standards and experienced managers and experts, the company is proud to have prepared the groundwork for this movement by its active presence in national projects and providing effective services. It hopes to continue this path by setting its mission, goals and proper management.

History

Nazerkararan Company was established in 1991 with the aim of providing engineering and inspection services. Since then, the company has been active in various fields including refineries, petrochemicals and gas.

Mission and Goals

Nazerkararan Company's goal is to provide inspection and engineering services with the highest quality and in accordance with national and international standards. With its experienced and specialized team, the company is committed to achieving prosperity and sustainable development.
